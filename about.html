About Me

I provide consultations and psychoanalytic sessions for individuals who wish to explore their unconscious, address persistent difficulties, or engage in a process of subjective transformation.

My Approach

  • Medical expertise in psychiatry: informed by evidence-based practice and clinical training.
  • Lacanian psychoanalysis: a rigorous method that privileges speech, listening, and the singularity of each subject.

I am committed to creating a space where speech can unfold freely, allowing each person to encounter their own truth and move beyond symptom-focused solutions.
