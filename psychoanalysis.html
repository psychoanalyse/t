Psychoanalysis

Psychoanalysis is a practice that privileges speech and listening, oriented to the singularity of each person. It is not about applying a technique to eliminate symptoms; rather, it opens a space for encountering what structures those symptoms and for subjective transformation.

What to Expect

  • Sessions are one-to-one and focus on your speech.
  • Frequency is tailored individually; some people attend weekly, others more or less often.
  • Duration may vary rather than being fixed to a standard 50 minutes; the orientation follows the logic of the session.
  • Work may involve anxiety, depression, obsessive thoughts/rituals, and questions of desire and meaning.
